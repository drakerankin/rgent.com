About Us

Rankin Gaming Entertainment is a video game development company. We are currently looking for dedicated artists and programmers to bring gaming to life, in a way the world has never seen it. For more information, Contact Us! If you are an artist, a portfolio will be required for consideration. There are a multitude of job openings here at RGENT, however, there is a certain level of expectation for the people who work at RGENT. If you aren't quite sure if you meet those standards, Check out our job listings! -Drake J. Rankin
